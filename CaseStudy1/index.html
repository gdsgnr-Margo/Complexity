About Me

I am Ma. Margaret S. Fundano, a passionate individual ready to delve into the captivating world of graphic design.


As a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science student, I merge technical prowess with creative finesse to craft visually stunning experiences. With three years of hands-on experience in graphic design, I've honed my skills across diverse projects, from dynamic logos to captivating clothing designs and polished publication materials. My portfolio boasts a spectrum of UI designs for both web and applications, showcasing my adaptability and versatility in the digital realm.


My journey in design is characterized by a relentless pursuit of excellence, fueled by a passion for innovation and a commitment to exceeding expectations.
